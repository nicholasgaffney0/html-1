Pisha Haja Bio

Pisha Haja, a band from Iceland, started back in 2016 when they first released their album Scottlish. Following the success of Scottlish, Pisha Haja kept themselves busy by putting out their next three albums in the same year. The highly anticipated follow up Unleash The Jeash met with glowing reviews and critical acclaim put the band to new heights. Pisha went back into the studio to record a short ep titled Thunder Road Sessions. Only a short four three months after, they released their most succesful album to date Turbisted and their highly successful single Pooch Tunes.


The release of Turbisted propelled Pisha Haja to international stardom.

Turbisted changed my life. If it wasn't for the song "I'm Going To Hawaii" I would have never quit my job to go on vacation to Hawaii, where I have been stranded for quite some time.
Some say the success of Turbisted has led to the next beatlemania type sensation: Pishamania.
